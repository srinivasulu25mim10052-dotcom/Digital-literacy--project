-> Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

This task focuses on identifying common financial cybercrimes in India and devloping a personal security framework. It includes both Part A and Part B.

Part A: It is the case study of a online payment fraud, where we deriveD the answer to the questions like:

i) What is cybercrime( online payment fraud)?

ii) How does it happen?

iii) Who are generally affected by it/ Who are the victims?

iv) What are the consequences?

Part B: It includes the prevention-checklist which can help us to act rationally in such cases. It also includes the reporting channel and helpline number.
